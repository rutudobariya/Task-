What are the Html Entities ? explain example two entities ?



Ans:

HTML entities are special codes used to represent characters that have a specific meaning in HTML or characters that are not easily typable on a keyboard. They are especially useful when you want to display reserved characters (such as <, >, &) or special characters (such as accented letters) on a webpage.
